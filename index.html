About Me

Hi, I'm Bern De Nijs, a game development student at Digital Arts and Entertainment (DAE) in Belgium. Projects

Patchwork Parkour

I worked on Patchwork Parkour as a lead designer and programmer. I was responsible for creating the climbing mechanic, several of the obstacles, and the multiplayer functionality. I was also a driving force behind the creative vision of the game. You can check out the game on itch.io.
